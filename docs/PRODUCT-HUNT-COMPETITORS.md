## Key Lessons From Winners

1. **Wispr Flow** — Launched TWICE. First for awareness, second for upgrade story. 20% paid conversion. **Lesson: Plan two launches.**
2. **ElevenLabs** — 16 launches in 2025. Each feature = its own PH moment. **Lesson: Every feature is a launch.**
3. **Aqua Voice** — "450ms" as headline metric. Tangible speed claim. **Lesson: Lead with a number.**
4. **OpenWhispr** — "Built it because I was tired of subscriptions." **Lesson: Maker story > feature list.**
5. **Klariqo** — PH-exclusive discount code drove 60 comments on 213 upvotes. **Lesson: PH-exclusive offers work.**
6. **Vapi** — AMA with founder + Series A announcement timed with launch. **Lesson: Pair milestones with launches.**

## What Wins on Product Hunt (2026 Algorithm)

- Comments > Upvotes (1 quality comment = 40-50 upvotes in weight)
- Video = 2.7x more upvotes
- First 4 hours are critical
- 400 engaged supporters > 4,000 cold contacts
- Featured status is everything — apply in advance

## Hardware Cautionary Tales

- **Humane AI Pin** — $230M raised, <10K shipped, bricked, sold for $116M. Over-promised.
- **Rabbit R1** — 100K sold day one, mass returns. Hardware mistakes are unfixable.
- **Lesson for VII:** Software-only is a STRENGTH. Can iterate. Ship what actually works.
